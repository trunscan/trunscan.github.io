What Is TRONSCAN?
TRONSCAN is a TRON-specific blockchain explorer that utilizes network data analysis. TRONSCAN allows users to read transactions, blocks, accounts, and smart contracts on the TRON network. Along with the information provided, TRONSCAN has a user-friendly interface that includes openness to network activities that allow users to immediately confirm transactions, see account balance details, and even check smart contracts. Creating and Managing a Wallet

Creating a Wallet
To connect the wallet, click the "Connect Wallet" button in the top-right corner.
Afterwards, you must choose TronLink and go through the setup process using the generated mnemonic phrase.
Save the given mnemonic phrase securely.

Importing an Existing Wallet
Go to TronLink, log in with your credentials, and then link your wallet.
After linking, you can check token balances and assets directly on TRONSCAN.

Monitoring Transactions
Enter the hash, wallet address, or block number in the search field to scrutinise a transaction's exact details.
After clicking the Search button, the transaction details will be fetched.
Examine the status of the transaction, the data of both the receiver/sender and the confirmation date of the transaction.

Analyzing Smart Contracts
Search for the address of the contract in the search field.
Check out the details, such as source code, decision history, and audit reports.
Ensure the contract has been certified by checking for the necessary certifications.
